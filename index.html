What We Do

Weekly Workshops

We hold bi-weekly, in-person and virtual workshop sessions on the 1st and 3rd Wednesdays of the month.

Conferences

We provide the opportunity to attend conferences as a club like Defcon and CactusCon.

Intraschool Networking

Monthly Joint Meetings with the ASU's hacking club on the 4th Wednesday of the month.

Trainings and Support

We provide trainings on tools and practices and a robust support group of students avaliable to answer questions via Discord.
